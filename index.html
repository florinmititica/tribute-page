Here's a timline of Louis Zamperini

  • Zamperini was born January 26, 1917
  • In 1936, Zamperini decided to try out for the Olympics
  • Zamperini could not get into what he did best, the 1500 meters, but he ran the 5,000 metres
  • At 19 years, 178 days, Zamperini is still the youngest American qualifier ever in the 5,000 meters
  • Zamperini enlisted in the United States Army Air Corps in September 1941
  • On their 47th day adrift, Zamperini and Phillips reached land in the Marshall Islands and were immediately captured by the Japanese Navy
  • Four days before his 81st birthday in January 1998, Zamperini ran a leg in the Olympic Torch relay for the Winter Olympics in Nagano, Japan
  • Zamperini's death came 70 years later, from pneumonia, on July 2, 2014, in Los Angeles, at home, aged 97
